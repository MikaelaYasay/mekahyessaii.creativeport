Insight

For this project, I designed a logo for Dubai of the United Arab Emirates. Upon research, Dubai is one of the most wealthiest countries in the world. The reason for its wealth, was the pearl industry. Pearl diving was the foundation and the growth of Dubai, and was the main source of income for many of the locals. Since then, Dubai has grown and built some astounding architecture. I wanted to capture that wealth, beauty, and the history of Dubai and how it became what it is today.
Dubai's Current State

Dubai is one of six emirates of the United Arab Emirates and in today's world, is well known for their abundant wealth, luxury shopping, lively nightlife, and state-of-the-art architecture such as the Burj Khalifa.

Noted History

It was once a small fishing village had a population of around 700-800 members of the Bani Yas tribe. Unlike its neighboring regions, oil is not their main source they focus on. For centuries, from the 1770’s to the late 1930’s, the UAE were reliant on the pearl industry to provide its income. Apart from growing wealthier in the pearl industry, it was the ceneter for the Gulf and African trade.
